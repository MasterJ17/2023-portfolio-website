About Jason Lopez

Jason Lopez is a 17 yo high school student from Delaware. He majors in the strings program at school, playing the cello. He is a student with a decent workload because he takes a couple of APs. Jason was also voted as the secretary of the Asian Student Association due to his dedication to the after school club during his Junior year. Outside of school, the majority of Jason's time is dedicated to Tiger Kicks. Tiger Kicks is the taekwondo school that he works at as an instructor. During his free time, he likes to workout, run, and even plays video games on occasion. When the weather is nice Jason likes to photosynthesize. Family and friends are very important to Jason. If someone needs him for something they can rely on him to be there for them.

Taekwondo

Jason Lopez Started his taekwondo journey very early in life, starting at the age of 5. He immidietly fell in love and knew that he wanted to keep going for many years to come. Although, he never imagined that he would one day be working along side his Masters to teach a new generation of kids that might one day be just like him.

Jason is so dedicated that every few years he has ranked up to his next black belt.

  • 3rd degree black belt: 12yo
  • 4th degree black belt: 17yo
